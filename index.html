Why Colombia Should Be on Your Bucket List

Discover amazing places, culture, and traditions

1. Visit Cartagena

Cartagena is a colorful city on the Caribbean coast. You should walk through its old town, full of history, music, and culture.

2. Cocora Valley

In Cocora Valley, you will see the tallest palm trees in the world. You should hike and enjoy breathtaking landscapes.

3. San Agustín

You should take a guided tour to learn about ancient statues and indigenous traditions. A guide would explain what each monument means.

4. Medellín

Medellín is called the “City of Eternal Spring”. You could ride the cable cars and visit Comuna 13 to see amazing street art.

5. Tayrona National Park

You should relax on white sand beaches and explore the tropical jungle full of wildlife.
