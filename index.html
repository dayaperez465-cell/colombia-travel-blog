Colombia Travel Bucket List

My Experience in the Condor Route

My Travel Experience

In 2024, I traveled with a group of bikers to the Condor Route. We visited the Piedra del Cóndor, the Valley of Frailejones, the Andulvio Lagoon, the Bedón Waterfall, the Tapir Watering Hole, and finally the San Juan Hot Springs. It was an unforgettable journey full of culture, adventure, and nature.

Where to Go

  • Piedra del Cóndor
  • Valley of Frailejones
  • Andulvio Lagoon
  • Bedón Waterfall
  • Tapir Watering Hole
  • San Juan Hot Springs

What to Do

  • Take a guided tour with the indigenous community
  • Observe the condor
  • Hike through nature trails
  • Admire waterfalls and lagoons
  • Relax in the hot springs

Travel Tips

  • You should book the tour in advance.
  • Bring comfortable clothes and hiking shoes.
  • Take water, snacks, and a camera.
  • Be respectful of nature and keep silence to observe the condor.
